Caesar Salad

A classic Caesar salad can fit into any healthy eating plan, but modifications to make it more nutritious may be a good idea if you eat them often. Choose a salad dressing that's low in saturated fat and sodium and consider using a 1-tablespoon (14-gram) serving.

Ingredients

  • Avacado - 2
  • Cucumber - 2
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
A classic Caesar salad can fit into any healthy eating plan, but modifications to make it more nutritious may be a good idea if you eat them often. Choose a salad dressing that's low in saturated fat and sodium and consider using a 1-tablespoon (14-gram) serving.

Procedure

  1. Cut the avacado,cucumber,tomato into small pieces. thinly slice the onion.

  2. In a mixing bowl, add diced avacado,tomato,cucumber,onions and cilantro.

  3. For dressing, add two table spoons of olive oil, squeeze a lemon and make the juice and combine both.

  4. Finally add dressing to mixture, add some salt and pepper as required and mix everything.
